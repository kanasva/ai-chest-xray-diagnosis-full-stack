# Facts

- This tool is not for medical use.
- It uses <a href="https://github.com/mlmed/torchxrayvision" target="_blank">TorchXRayVision</a> as an AI model to diagnose 18 chest X-ray pathologies, including Atelectasis, Cardiomegaly, Consolidation, Edema, Effusion, Emphysema, Enlarged Cardiomediastinum, Fibrosis, Fracture, Hernia, Infiltration, Lung Lesion, Lung Opacity, Mass, Nodule, Pleural Thickening, Pneumonia, and Pneumothorax.
- For more information, visit <a href="https://github.com/kanasva/ai-chest-xray-diagnosis-full-stack" target="_blank">AI Chest X-ray Diagnosis Full-stack</a> and <a href="https://github.com/kanasva/ai-chest-xray-diagnosis-api" target="_blank">Chest X-ray Diagnosis API</a>.

## Privacy

- Upon signing in, your name, email, and avatar URI are stored solely for authentication purposes. No other data, such as X-ray images or diagnoses, is stored.
